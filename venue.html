Venue

Fazekas (contest venue)

The high school, which is the hungarian leader school of IIOT, is a more than 100-year-old inner-city school. Dozens of international science olympiads and other competitors have graduated from this school over the years. Its mathematics department is world-renowned. The opening and closing ceremonies will be held in the Great Hall (Nagyterem). The IIOT finals will be held in its computer labs.
https://fazekas.hu/

Hotel

Accommodation will be in the Ibis Styles Budapest City hotel on the banks of the Danube, near the Grand Boulevard (Google Maps). Double rooms will be available with wifi access.

You can start your day with a standard buffet breakfast including Hungarian specialties (06:00 - 10:00).

Small shops and a variety of restaurants are located near the hotel.

The accommodation is within walking distance of the contest venue, but also accessible by public transport (3+1 stops).

Hungary information

Currency: Hungarian Forint (HUF)

The majority of shops and restaurants accept credit cards. More information.

Power plugs: 220V, Type F (Schuko) or Type C (same as most of Europe). More information.

Weather: At the end of May and beginning of June, Hungary typically enjoys warm late spring weather, gradually transitioning into summer. Daytime highs: 23–30°C, Nighttime lows: 10–15°C. Often sunny or partly cloudy, but occasional showers or thunderstorms are common.

See weather forecast before traveling.
Local weather forecast with more details: idokep.hu

Transportation: The public transportations system is good in Budapest. Route planning is available in Google Maps. Travel passes will be provided by the organizers to everyone.

General information about Budapest: Budapest Wikipedia
